Rio de Janeiro

Rio de Janeiro is a large city in Brazil located by the sea. It is known for its beaches of Copacabana and Ipanema, its statue of Christ the Redeemer 38 meters high located at the top of Corcovado and the Sugar Loaf, granite peak whose summit is accessible via 2 cable cars.
